About

A Bird on the Wind is a bird who becomes one with the natural current; who glides on pathways with or without navigation. It is to let oneself be guided, rather than control the reins. To spread one’s wings and be taken by whichever breeze blows most is to take joy in the journey and be led to new destinations. A bird who flaps and flaps and flaps all through the day is a bird of ego, of frivolity; a false independent. Let us then be birds on the wind; let us fly by way of Mother Nature into new channels; let us revel in the curiosity of the new, and be challenged for what we believe to be true.
